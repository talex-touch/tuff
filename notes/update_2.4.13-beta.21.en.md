# Tuff v2.4.13-beta.21 Release Notes

## Highlights

- New voice dictation: a global shortcut turns speech into text in real time, with read-aloud playback.
- Smoother updates: downloads in the background, installs and restarts in one click, with silent updates on macOS.

## Known Limitations

- Voice dictation is experimental; recognition and transcription quality are still being refined — feedback welcome.
